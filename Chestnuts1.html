Poem for children "Chestnuts" by Olga Kobylska

What nice green hedgehogs
Sit on he trees in the shadows?
They are small chestnuts

They take off their spikes
If the wind blows.
They smile and wink to me so cute
Then they fall down
And bounce like balls.
_____________________________
Translated by Helen Kapshytar
